Where the hell is my class? (October and November)

A page to help you know where and when your classes are while your Synapses accouont is being created. Please remember that looking at the official website's time table requiers logging in.

This page's source code is publically available at this link. Additionally, this page does not use cookies to store any data.

Helpful key:

  • (L): Lectures take place here
  • (C): Course work takes place here
  • (dd/mm): For that day, the room is changed to this one
  • (T): Takes place at the Telecom-Paris building
  • (P): Takes place at the Polytechnique building
